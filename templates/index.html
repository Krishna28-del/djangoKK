Disaster Management Information Portal

Welcome to the Disaster Management Information Portal. This site provides essential safety tips and guidelines to help you prepare for and respond to various disasters.

Types of Disasters

  • Earthquakes: Learn how to stay safe before, during, and after an earthquake.
  • Floods: Discover preparation and survival tips for floods.
  • Hurricanes: Find out how to prepare for hurricanes and emergency response measures.
  • Fires: Get fire safety tips and learn about evacuation plans.
  • Pandemics: Follow health guidelines and preventive measures during pandemics.

Emergency Contact Information

  • Fire Department: 911
  • Local Police: 911
  • Disaster Management Authority: 1-800-DISASTER

General Safety Tips

  • Keep an emergency kit with essential supplies.
  • Know the evacuation routes in your area.
  • Stay informed by listening to the news and official alerts.
  • Have a communication plan with your family.
